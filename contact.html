About Me

In 2013, I had a family and financial situation in which I had to put my film industry career on pause, BUT! Now I am back and ready to achieve my dreams.

I have always had a passion for producing films.  I love to oversee the film production from pre-production to post production. I enjoy the planning aspects of film production, such as selecting the script; coordinating writing, development, arranging financing and marketing.

I love to work with a diverse range of people and can communicate with cast and crew. I love Australia and working all over this amazing country and meeting people. I would love to work in documentary but am flexible with all genres of films within the industry.

I would like to become a production manager/producer of a short film or documentary that will get funding to become a feature.
