What I bring to every production!

Communication

Excellent communication is vital to any production. From emails, call sheets, texts, phone calls to all crew and departments I guarantee with me you will always be up to date regarding any production matters.

Negotiation

Whether it be negotiating location agreements, securing funding, equipment or crew, face-to-face or ear-to-ear, I am sure to articulate concluded issues and get acknowledgement from the other side that all  are correct for a smooth production.

Ambition

With a strong desire to  achieve the best quality production possible my ambition and attitude is sure to extend to all those of whom I work with. I am persistent, ambitious, passionate, with a great attitude who loves to learn and work with a diverse range of people

Admin

Production office duties. Looking after all logs and deliverables. Ensuing all release forms are in order, signed and collected and adhering to all procedures.

Knowledge

Garnering the tacit knowledge and explicit knowledge through my years of study and real production experience. I can promise you I am up to speed and always eager to extend and learn more.

Flexibility

No matter the challenge or problem I am flexible and open to changing scenarios. I can work any day, any week, dedication 100% of my time to your production.
